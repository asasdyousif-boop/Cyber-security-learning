NDG Linux Essentials -chapter 13 -
Where Data is Stored
----------------------------------

ps

Shows running processes.

Example:
ps

---

ps aux

Shows all running processes with detailed information.

Example:
ps aux

---

top

Displays real-time system processes and resource usage.

Example:
top

---

free

Shows memory usage (RAM).

Example:
free

---

dmesg

Displays system messages (kernel logs).

Example:
dmesg

---

su -

Switches to another user (usually root) with full environment.

Example:
su -
